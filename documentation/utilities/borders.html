Border radius

Add classes to an element to easily round its corners.

  • Preview
  • HTML 
                        
                          <img class="rounded" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-top" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-right" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-bottom" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-left" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-circle" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-pill" src="..." alt="...">
                          <img class="rounded-0" src="..." alt="...">
                          <div class="bg-img-hero gradient-x-overlay-sm-navy rounded-pseudo" style="background-image: url(../../assets/img/900x450/img1.jpg);"></div>

Important

.rounded-pseudo border radius class used on top of images that are overlayed with background gradients.
